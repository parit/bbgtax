Our Services

Providing best taxation services while creating long term relationships with our clients

Tax Advisory services

We help you to take business decisions which are in line with the current tax laws in force in India. We can also help you to design and structure complex transactions in the most tax efficient manner.

Transfer pricing Advisory

We provide Transfer pricing advisory before an International Transaction has been entered between the Associated Enterprises. It involves Transfer Pricing Planning, Price Setting Mechanism and Policy Formation including implementation.

Transfer pricing Complaince

We help in preparing of annual documentation/filings with the tax office including the Master File, Country by Country Reporting, Transfer Pricing Report, Form 3CEB.

Dispute resolution and Tax litigation

With the faceless assessment & appeal it has become imperative to defend the case with the best in class submissions. Offlate the revenue authorities are focusing more widely and intensely on transfer pricing issues. We can help in effective dispute resolution across all levels.

International Taxation

We provide wide range of Tax compliance, certification and consulting services for our international clients.

Arbitration and Mediation

Being a certified Arbitrator and a certified commercial Mediator we have helped various clients resolve their disputes amicably without going to court. We are passionate about dispute resolution and strives hard to help clients resolve their disputes.
