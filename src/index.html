Tax Advisory services

We help you to take business decisions which are in line with the current tax laws in force in India. We can also help you to design and structure complex transactions in the most tax efficient manner.

Transfer pricing Advisory

We provide Transfer pricing advisory before an International Transaction has been entered between the Associated Enterprises. It involves Transfer Pricing Planning, Price Setting Mechanism and Policy Formation including implementation.

Transfer pricing Complaince

We help in preparing of annual documentation/filings with the tax office including the Master File, Country by Country Reporting, Transfer Pricing Report, Form 3CEB.
